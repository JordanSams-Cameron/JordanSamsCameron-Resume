Jordan Sams-Cameron

Golf Professional

I am a current student athlete at the University of California, Riverside where I major in Philosophy. I am currently in my second year of playing golf for the UCR Highlanders. I have strived to achieve the goal of playing Division I athletics for many years and have been able to achieve this through much perserverance and determination. Through this journey I have been able to acquire the knowledge and skill to propel me into an elite division of the golf world, not only as a player but as a teacher as well.

I have worked in the golf industry for nearly 10 years, from a Golf Cart attendant to Pro Shop attendant to Golf Teacher, enjoying every moment of it. The ability to help others who strive to improve their golf skills is very rewarding for myself. I have been able to help and teach many people through my work in the golf industry, starting with merely providing tips from the Pro Shop to our regular customers, to providing multi-session, hour long private lessons to one individual.

Golf has not only provided many life lessons and personal motivation, but has helped me become a successful individual. I plan to use this ability in the future to seek a professional golf career in either teaching or playing the game for many years to come.
